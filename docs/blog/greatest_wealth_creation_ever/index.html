The Greatest Wealth Creation Ever

We’re living in a time of the greatest creation of wealth the world has ever seen. And who is responsible for this? The politician? The bankers? The MBAs?

No, it’s the nerds.

Let’s take a look at what this motley bunch studied at University:

Jeff Bezos: Electrical Engineering and Computing
Sergey Brin (Google): Computer Science and Mathematics
Bill Gates: Mathematics and Computer Science
Elon Musk: Physics
Larry Page (Google): Computer Engineering
Steve Wozniak (Apple): Electrical Engineering and Computer Science

Now, you might not like them, you might not like their politics but you cannot deny that there is a strong correlation between being a self-made billionaire and being a geek.

So, why do we in the West view them with such contempt?
